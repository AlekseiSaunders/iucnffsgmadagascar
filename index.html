Freshwater fish are some of the most endangered vertebrates in Madagascar

We are going to change that

The Madagascarfish website provides the data needed to collaborate, conserve, and repatriate Madagascar's native freshwater ichthyofauna.

As a group of ichthyologists, aquarists, and enthusiasts we are working together to secure a future in which Madagascar's freshwater fish fauna persist in their natural habitats.
