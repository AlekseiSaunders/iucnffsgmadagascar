Killifish

The family Aplocheilidae currently holds 16 species within two genera and can be found in Asia, Madagascar,and the Seychelles. Only the genera Pachypanchax, currently with six described species, is found on Madagascar.

Rainbowfish

The subfamily Bedotiinae (of the family Melanotaeniidae) consists of two genera (Bedotia and Rheocles) and 16 species. Bedotia and Rheocles are endemic to Madagascar.

Cichlids

The family Cichlidae is large and diverse with at least 251 genera and 1743 species. Madagascar has 29 described species across 6 genera - Katria, Oxylapia, Paratilapia, Paretroplus, Ptychochromis, and Ptychochromoides.

Catfish

The family Anchariidae is endemic to Madagascar with two genera and six currently recognized species.

Gobies

The family Eleotridae consists of 34 genera and 180 species. Madagascar has four genera and nine species.

Round Herring

The family Clupidae consists mainly of marine species. Two species in the genus Sauvagella and one in Spratellomorpha are endemic to the freshwaters of Madagascar and are important food fish.
