Aleksei Saunders

Aleksei Saunders is a Canadian ichthyologist residing in Colorado, USA.

He has worked with native freshwater and marine fishes in Canada, the United States of America, and Madagascar. He specializes in habitat assessments, habitat loss mitigation, ex-situ husbandry, and long-term ex-situ breeding programs. Alex has a Bachleor's of Biology from Simon Fraser University.
