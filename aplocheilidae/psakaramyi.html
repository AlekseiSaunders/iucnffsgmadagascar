Conservation

Conservation Efforts:

This species is currently in conservation breeding programs in North American and Europe.

This species is thought to occur in Lac Texier and one other low altitude crater lake. These lakes are located inside the boundaries of Montagne d’Ambre National Park and may offer some protection to the species.

These habitats have not been sampled since the late 1950s and nothing is known of the current status of these subpopulations.

Husbandry

This species has not been kept in captivity.
