Species Details

Species Summary

    Collection Locations:

  • Type location - Northeastern Madagascar, Mahajanga Province, near town of Mandritsara, Sofia drainage basin, Amboaboa (Ambomboa) River.
  • Location: -15.83 °S, 48.71 °E

Species Discussion

Described in Stiassny, M., and Sparks, J. S. (2006). Phylogeny and Taxonomic Revision of the Endemic Malagasy Genus Ptychochromis (Teleostei: Cichlidae), with the Description of Five New Species and a Diagnosis for Katria, New Genus. - American Museum novitates, no. 3535

Conservation Efforts

Ptychochromis insolitus is currently being pond bred by the Association des Producteurs Privés d'Alevins d'Andapa (APPA) with the support of:

  • Fish Net Madagascar
  • The European Union of Aquarium Curators (EUAC)
  • The American Cichlid Association (ACA)
  • Toronto Zoo
  • Zoological Society London(ZSL)

Ex-situ breeding populations also exist in Zoological institutions such as Toronto Zoo, ZSL, and Cologne Zoo.

Ptychochromis insolitus is also becoming more available to enthusiasts through a Citizen Conservation Program in Europe and Below Water in Canada.
